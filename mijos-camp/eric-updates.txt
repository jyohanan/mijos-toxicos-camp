CAMP STRUCTURE

FOOTBALL CAMP
Morning Session
Check-In: 7:30 AM
Programming: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM

SOCCER CAMP
Afternoon Session
Check-In: 12:30 PM
Programming: 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM

We should also add a note that athletes register for either Football or Soccer based on their primary sport.

CALL TO ACTION

After the “Mijo Experience” section, I’d like to place another registration call-to-action.

At that point, visitors should have enough information to make a registration decision, and we should give them an opportunity to act before continuing down the page.
