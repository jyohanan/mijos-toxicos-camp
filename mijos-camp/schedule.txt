Football

MiJO × Chicos Tóxicos Youth Camp Schedule
Join us for a day of competition, community, culture, and unforgettable experiences alongside athletes, coaches, influencers, and special guests.

7:30 AM – Athlete Check-In Opens
Athletes and families arrive for registration, credential verification, and event check-in. Families are encouraged to arrive early to ensure a smooth check-in experience before opening ceremonies begin.

9:00 AM – Opening Ceremony
The day officially begins with welcome remarks, guest speakers, athlete introductions, sponsor acknowledgements, and an overview of the day’s activities.

9:25 AM – Transition to the Field
Athletes will move from the staging area to the field in preparation for the day’s competitions and activities.

9:30 AM – Dynamic Warm-Up
All athletes will participate in a camp-wide warm-up led by coaches, trainers, and special guests to prepare for the day’s challenges.

9:50 AM – Competition Stations
Athletes will rotate through a series of competition-based stations designed to challenge speed, athleticism, teamwork, and overall performance.

Competition Station Highlights
Athletic Challenges
Team Competitions
Speed Challenges
Agility Challenges
Head-to-Head Matchups
Group Competitions
Athletes will rotate through multiple stations while competing alongside fellow campers, coaches, and special guests.

11:00 AM – Competition Challenges
The entire camp comes together for a series of exciting large-group competitions and showcase challenges.

Challenge Highlights
Influencers vs. Campers
Fastest Camper Competition
40-Yard Dash Challenge
Tug-of-War Battles
Team Challenges
Special Competitions & Giveaways
Participants will have opportunities to compete, earn bragging rights, and create memorable moments throughout the event.

12:00 PM – Closing Ceremony
Athletes, families, coaches, and guests gather for closing remarks, athlete recognition, sponsor acknowledgements, and final announcements.

12:30 PM – Photos, Food & Meet-and-Greet
Athletes and families will be directed through the event activation area where they can:

Take photos with special guests
Meet coaches and influencers
Visit sponsor activations
Enjoy food and refreshments
Connect with community partners
1:00 PM – Event Concludes
Thank you for joining us for an unforgettable day of competition, community, and culture.

1:30 PM – Facility Reset
The football field will be fully cleared and prepared for afternoon programming.
